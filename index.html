QR codes can point anywhere. Here’s how to stay safe next time:

• Preview the URL before opening. If it’s shortened or weird, don’t proceed.
• Don’t enter passwords/payment after scanning a public QR—with Wi-Fi portals especially.
• Watch for requests to install apps, profiles, or “security tools.” Close the page.
• If something pops up full-screen and urgent, back out. Visit the site manually instead.
